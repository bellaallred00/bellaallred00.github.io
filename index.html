Why Superman is the greatest Superhero

Superman is the greatest superhero because with his many super powers he can save multiple people at a time.

He was also always saving Lois Lane when people threatened her becuase she knew him. She was never able to get hurt because of him but he eventually died.

The super villian Doomsday ended up killing him and he was later revived by the justice league.
